## Summary of Polishing Approach

**What Gets Removed:**
- Timestamps `[0:00-0:00]`
- Station call letters, broadcast times, disclaimers
- Filler words ("and such", "you know")
- Cross-talk fragments and incomplete sentences
- Unnecessary conversational back-and-forth

**What Gets Fixed:**
- Speaker labels (A/B/C → actual names)
- Transcription errors
- Run-on sentences
- Fragmented thoughts from interruptions

**What Gets Added:**
- Section headers (before host question that introduces topic)
- 3-5 searchable tags per section
- Paragraph breaks (3-4 sentences max)

**What Stays 100% The Same:**
- All facts, figures, dates, names, studies
- Dr. Peat's voice, phrasing, and authentic speaking style
- Sequence and logic of discussion
- Credibility markers and qualifications
